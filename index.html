Tribute to our founder

Created after our founder's initials, the TB monogram symbolizes our continued connection with Thomas Burberry. The latter was born in 1835 and founded Burberry at the age of just 21. Considered a visionary designer, he is the originator of gabardine, the iconic fabric making up our Heritage trench coats.
